About me

Fashion designer: Zorana Petrovic,

Born on 10.08.1987. in Belgrade

Education:

  • Leather product designer [shoes, haberdashery and clothing] (2010.),
    Bachelor degree on the "Belgrade Polytechnic" college, Belgrade.
  • Technician- clothes pattern maker (2006.), Textile design high school, Belgrade.

Designers motto:

"Having style does not mean to be seen but to be remembered."

"Zazi":

Unique clothing items that are designed for strong, confident individuals, with distinctive attitude, who want to be noticed and remembered as different from others.
